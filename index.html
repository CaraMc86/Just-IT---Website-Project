ARCADE

Tottenham Court Road

Situated in the iconic Centre Point building, Arcade Tottenham Court Road houses open kitchens that travel the world, from Middle Eastern shawarma to US-style smash burgers. The standalone cocktail bar, Centre Point Bar, serves drinks using fresh market ingredients to mirror the flavours of the kitchens. The mezzanine level is home to Luke Farrell's flagship Southern Thai comfort food restaurant, Plaza Khao Gaeng.
